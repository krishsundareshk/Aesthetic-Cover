Her Eyes

Are a divine sanctuary, two deep and wonderful worlds where I gladly lose my sense of time. There is a profound honesty in their depth—a quiet, shimmering kindness that feels more like home. I could spend an eternity staring into them, forever curious, forever captivated by the way they convey a thousand unspoken emotions in a single glance. Even the playful, cute way she rolls them is a treasure to me, a small spark of the spirit I so deeply admire. They are mirrors of her soul, filled to the brim with a love so pure it humbles me. To read her eyes is to know a truth more beautiful than any poet could write, and I find myself endlessly wanting more of the warmth that lives behind them.
