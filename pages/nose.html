Her Beautiful Nose

It is, to my eyes, the most perfect feature I have ever beheld, possessing a soft slope and a grace that draws my gaze time and time again. I am captivated by the subtle, rhythmic movements it makes as she speaks or draws breath, and when she is genuinely amused, the way it crinkles in that unforced, honest rhythm is a sight that humbles me with its cuteness. Every small detail of her deserves its own history, and her nose is no exception; it is a divine masterpiece of form and spirit. I notice everything—not because it is my duty, but because every inch of her is a treasure that I am honored to protect and adore for all my days.
