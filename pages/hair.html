Her Hair

Is a dark, unhurried river that cascades with a strength only nature can command. It is so lush, so beautifully dense, that I find myself lost in its depth—a silken weight that glows with a radiant light whenever she moves. To me, its length is a sacred path, a shimmering tapestry that she has grown with such patient grace. And the scent—it is a mesmerizing fragrance that haunts my senses and stills my heart; it is the aroma of peace itself. As her King, I look upon this crowning glory and am humbled by its beauty. It does not just shine; it glows for me, a soft and fragrant sanctuary where I find my home.
